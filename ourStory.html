Our Story

Daisy Priya, Founder

I’m Daisy Priya, the founder of *Chosen Lamb*. I started this journey with a simple goal—to create beautiful, sustainable products that don’t break the bank. I’ve always believed that luxury and affordability shouldn’t be opposites, and that’s why I focus on crafting high-quality, eco-friendly candles that everyone can enjoy. Sustainability is at the heart of everything I do, from the materials I choose to the way each product is made. With *Chosen Lamb*, I want to bring warmth, comfort, and a touch of elegance to your space while staying true to my commitment to the planet.

Inspired by Hobbies

My love for crafting and creating unique items has always been a significant part of my life. This passion for handmade crafts inspired me to start making candles. Each candle is a reflection of my dedication to quality and creativity.

Through this journey, I have learned the importance of sustainability and the joy of bringing warmth and light into people's lives.

Our Goals

At Chosen Lamb, our goal is to create high-quality, sustainable candles that bring warmth and joy to every home. We aim to expand our product line, reach more customers, and continue to innovate in the world of handmade crafts.

We are committed to sustainability, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Join us on this journey and experience the magic of handmade candles.
